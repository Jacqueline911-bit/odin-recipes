Beef Stir-Fry

Desciption:

This sizzling beef stir-fry is a mouthwatering dish that combines tender strips of beef with colorful vegetables and a savory sauce.
The beef is marinated to infuse it with flavors and then quickly stir-fried to perfection, resulting in a delightful blend of textures and tastes.
Whether you're preparing a weeknight dinner for your family or hosting a dinner party, this beef stir-fry is sure to impress with its enticing aroma and tantalizing flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) beef sirloin or flank steak, thinly sliced into strips
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (divided)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally (for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and sesame oil to make the marinade. Add the sliced beef to the marinade, making sure it's evenly coated.
    Let it marinate for at least 20 minutes.
  2. Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a wok or a large skillet over high heat.
    Once the oil is hot, add the marinated beef and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes or until it's browned.
    Remove the beef from the wok and set it aside.
  3. In the same wok, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil and saut√© the minced garlic and grated ginger until fragrant, for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add the sliced red and green bell peppers, onion, and broccoli to the wok. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.
  5. In a small bowl, mix the hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, and honey to create the sauce. Pour the sauce over the vegetables in the wok and stir to coat them evenly.
  6. Return the cooked beef to the wok and toss everything together, allowing the flavors to meld for another minute.
  7. Transfer the beef stir-fry to a serving platter. Garnish with sliced green onions and sprinkle sesame seeds on top for an extra touch of flavor and presentation.

    8. Serve the beef stir-fry hot with steamed rice or noodles for a delightful and satisfying meal.
