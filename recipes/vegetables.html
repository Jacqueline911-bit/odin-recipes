Vegetable stir fry

Desciption:

Vegetable Stir-Fry is a quick and colorful dish that showcases the natural flavors of a variety of vegetables.
This recipe allows you to use your favorite vegetables or whatever you have on hand.
The vegetables are stir-fried in a savory sauce, resulting in a delicious and nutritious meal that's perfect for a weeknight dinner or a side dish to accompany your main course.

Desciption:

  • 1 lb (450g) beef sirloin or flank steak, thinly sliced into strips
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup sugar snap peas or snow peas
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup sliced cabbage
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce (optional for added flavor)
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds and sliced green onions (for garnish)
  • Cooked rice or noodles (to serve)

Steps:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce (if using), rice vinegar, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Set the sauce aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced red and yellow bell peppers, broccoli florets, carrot, sugar snap peas or snow peas, sliced mushrooms, and cabbage to the skillet.
    Stir-fry the vegetables for about 3-4 minutes or until they are slightly tender but still crisp.
  4. Pour the prepared sauce over the vegetables in the skillet. Toss everything together to coat the vegetables evenly with the sauce.
    Continue cooking for an additional 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the vegetables.
  5. Season the stir-fry with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference.
  6. Remove the skillet from the heat. The Vegetable Stir-Fry is now ready to be served.
  7. Serve the Vegetable Stir-Fry over cooked rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions for an extra touch of flavor and presentation.

    8. Enjoy this delightful and colorful Vegetable Stir-Fry as a delicious and healthy meal or a delightful side dish!
