Chicken stir Parmesan

Desciption:

Chicken Parmesan is a classic Italian-American dish that features tender breaded chicken cutlets topped with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese.
This comforting and flavorful dish is perfect for a family dinner or a special gathering. Serve it over spaghetti or with a side of garlic bread for a complete and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (Italian-style if available)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it with olive oil.
  2. Trim any excess fat from the chicken breasts and pound them to an even thickness (about 1/2 inch) with a meat mallet or the back of a heavy skillet.
    Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. In three separate shallow dishes, set up a breading station. In the first dish, add the all-purpose flour. In the second dish, beat the eggs.
    In the third dish, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, dried basil, garlic powder, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour, shaking off any excess. Then dip the chicken into the beaten eggs, ensuring it's fully coated.
    Finally, press the chicken into the breadcrumb mixture, pressing down gently to adhere the breadcrumbs to the chicken. Make sure the chicken is evenly coated.
  5. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the breaded chicken breasts and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side or until the breadcrumbs are golden brown and the chicken is cooked through.
    You may need to do this in batches if your skillet is not large enough to fit all the chicken at once.
  6. Transfer the cooked chicken breasts to the prepared baking sheet. Spoon a generous amount of marinara sauce over each chicken breast, spreading it evenly.
    Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  7. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove the Chicken Parmesan from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

    9. Serve the Chicken Parmesan over cooked spaghetti or with a side of garlic bread for a delightful and satisfying Italian-inspired meal! Enjoy!
